# RacSogMo Protocol: Eligible Clearing Provider Requirements

## 🛡️ Core Philosophy (核心理念)
The RacSogMo middleware is an open-source, AI-native routing protocol. To ensure the absolute safety of funds and regulatory compliance for our AI Agent ecosystem, RacSogMo **does not** hold or clear funds directly. 

Instead, the protocol rigorously mandates that all underlying TWD (New Taiwan Dollar) transactions must be routed exclusively through **Tier-1 Licensed Payment Gateways** in Taiwan that pass our strict compliance matrix.

## ⚔️ The "Hero's Trial": Mandatory Compliance Matrix
To be whitelisted as an official clearing provider for the RacSogMo protocol, a financial institution must meet the following non-negotiable criteria:

### 1. Regulatory Registration (數位發展部能量登錄)
The provider must have officially passed the **Third-Party Payment Service Provider Energy Registration (第三方支付服務業能量登錄)** mandated by the Ministry of Digital Affairs (MODA) in Taiwan. Unregistered entities are strictly blocked at the protocol level.

### 2. Bank Escrow & Trust (價金信託與履約保證)
100% of the processed funds must be secured by a verified Taiwanese bank trust or strict performance guarantee mechanism. 

### 3. Enterprise-Grade AML & Law Enforcement Liaison (洗錢防制與警調對接)
The provider must possess a robust internal auditing team capable of stringent merchant onboarding (KYC/KYB). Furthermore, they must have established, high-efficiency liaison channels with Taiwanese banks, local police, and investigation bureaus to intercept fraudulent AI-driven transactions.

### 4. Zero-Latency Infrastructure (AI 級基礎設施)
Legacy providers with slow redirect times are incompatible. The provider must support highly concurrent API responses to prevent LLM timeout errors.
